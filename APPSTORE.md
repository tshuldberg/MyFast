## Long Description

MyFast is an intermittent fasting timer built for people who want a great app without a subscription.

Pay once. Use forever. No recurring charges, no trials that auto-renew, no "premium" tiers.

**What you get for $4.99:**

- One-tap fasting timer with a clean, animated progress ring
- Preset protocols: 16:8, 18:6, 20:4, OMAD, and 5:2
- Live countdown with target time and remaining duration
- Full fasting history organized by month
- Streak tracking to build consistency
- Weekly chart showing your fasting pattern
- Stats dashboard: current streak, longest streak, total fasts, average duration
- Optional weight logging in lbs or kg
- Home screen widget showing your active fast
- Export your data as CSV anytime
- Dark theme designed for nighttime use

**What you don't get:**

- No subscriptions
- No ads
- No accounts or sign-ups
- No data collection or tracking
- No upsells or locked features

All your data stays on your device in a local database. MyFast doesn't request network permissions -- it literally cannot send your data anywhere.

Built for people who fast, not for people who sell subscriptions.
