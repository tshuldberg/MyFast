# Privacy Policy

**MyFast** -- Intermittent Fasting Timer

*Last updated: February 22, 2026*

## Summary

MyFast collects zero data. Your fasting history stays on your device and never leaves it.

## Data Collection

MyFast does **not** collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers. Specifically:

- **No analytics.** No usage tracking, no event logging, no telemetry of any kind.
- **No crash reporting.** The app does not send crash reports or diagnostics.
- **No advertising.** No ad SDKs, no ad identifiers, no tracking pixels.
- **No accounts.** No email, no sign-up, no login. The app does not ask for or store any personal information.
- **No network permissions.** MyFast does not request network access. The app cannot transmit data because it has no ability to connect to the internet.

## Data Storage

All data -- fasting history, weight entries, streaks, and settings -- is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. This data is included in your device backups (iCloud or local) according to your device settings.

## Third-Party SDKs

MyFast does **not** include any third-party SDKs that collect data. The app uses:

- **Expo / React Native** -- open-source framework for building the app
- **expo-sqlite** -- local database storage on your device

None of these transmit data to external servers.

## Data Deletion

You can erase all app data at any time from Settings > Erase All Data. You can also delete the app, which removes all stored data from your device.

## Children's Privacy

MyFast does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.

## Open Source

MyFast is source-available under the FSL-1.1-Apache-2.0 license. You can inspect the source code to verify these privacy claims.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
